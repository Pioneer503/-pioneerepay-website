When you process payments through our Service, we collect transaction data, including: • Transaction amount, date, and time • Card information (last 4 digits only; we never store full card numbers) • Merchant and customer information related to transactions • Payment method and status • Refund and dispute information

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service: • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system) • Usage data (pages visited, features accessed, time spent) • Cookies and similar tracking technologies • Location information (approximate geographic location)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes: • Providing and improving the Service • Processing payments and transactions • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations • Detecting and preventing fraud • Customer support and communication • Marketing and promotional activities (with your consent) • Analytics and service optimization • Protecting our rights and the rights of our users

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information, including: • PCI DSS Level 1 compliance for payment card data • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data in transit and at rest • Secure authentication protocols (OAuth 2.0, two-factor authentication) • Regular security audits and penetration testing • Access controls limiting employee access to personal information • Incident response procedures for data breaches

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years to comply with federal regulations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with: • Payment networks (Visa, Mastercard, etc.) as required for transaction processing • Financial institutions and third-party payment processors • Law enforcement when required by law • Service providers who assist with our operations (under confidentiality agreements) • Business partners for joint offerings (only with your consent)

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to: • Access your personal information • Correct inaccurate information • Request deletion of your information • Object to processing of your information • Opt-out of marketing communications • Data portability
